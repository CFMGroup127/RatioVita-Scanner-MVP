RatioVita Next Generation - Comprehensive Development Prompt
Executive Summary
You are tasked with creating a completely new, clean, and robust version of RatioVita - a comprehensive life management application that serves as a digital filing cabinet, receipt scanner, asset tracker, and business management tool. This is a complete rebuild from scratch, incorporating all lessons learned from the previous generation while avoiding the build errors and architectural issues that plagued the original implementation.
What is RatioVita?
RatioVita (Latin: "Reason for Life" or "Purpose of Life") is a comprehensive life management application designed to be the central hub for organizing, tracking, and managing all aspects of personal and professional life. It serves as a digital filing cabinet, receipt scanner, asset tracker, inventory manager, and business management tool.
Core Purpose
Digital Life Organization: Centralized management of all life documents, receipts, assets, and information
Business Operations: Support for independent contractors, small businesses, and production companies
Financial Management: Receipt tracking, expense categorization, tax preparation, and financial planning
Asset Lifecycle Management: Complete tracking from purchase to disposal, including maintenance and warranties
Production Industry Support: Specialized features for film/TV production, costume design, and creative industries
