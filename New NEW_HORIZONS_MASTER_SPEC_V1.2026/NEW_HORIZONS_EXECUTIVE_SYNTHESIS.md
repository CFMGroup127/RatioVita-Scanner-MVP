## Production model

- **Season** = one week (one guest kitchen).
- **Episode** = one day.
- **Sunday finale** = exclusive one-night menu.
- **Crew taxonomy:** film roles (EP, Showrunner, DP/chef, Grips, Cast, PAs, Greens).
- **Scenic:** overhead truss + booth-anchored fly walls; no screws in heritage terrazzo.
- **Waste:** Marché absorbs flagship surplus; vermicompost → rooftop farm.
