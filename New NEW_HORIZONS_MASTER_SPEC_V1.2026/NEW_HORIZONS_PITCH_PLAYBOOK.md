# New Horizons — pitch playbook (Charles Khabouth / CF)

## What to send before the meeting

**Send:** Typed note + one glossy **Episodic Stack** cutaway + macro page ($125M, 350k OTPP, $45–65 ASP).

**Do not send:** Full Production Bible (IP exposure; weakens live reveal).

> Ink is burning fixed overhead on dark mid-week real estate while weekend bottle service compresses.  
> Enclosed: macro profile for a 7-day, PATH-anchored civic engine at **176 Yonge** — modeled **$125M+** gross on accessible check averages, with a **zero-cost OTPP member pipeline** that pre-sells capacity.  
> Full operational blueprints are in our Production Bible. I am selecting a **co–Executive Producer**, not a passive investor.  
> **15 minutes, [day].** I will bring the Bible; you bring the greenlight.  
> — [Name]

## In-room sequence (15–45 min)

1. **NDA** — mutual; trade secrets schedule (kitchen tracks, pension algorithm, app).
2. **Rebel questions** — “How often is Rebel open?” / revenue shape — **do not ask for numbers**; let him compute.
3. **Pivot** — “What do you know about film production? I need a co–EP.”
4. **Open Bible** — vertical stack → PATH breakfast → Marché → 52-week theatre → fleet/Rebel → RatioVita → media → deal memo ranges.
5. **Close** — OTPP + industry + ACTRA discounts; fleet Hold/Fire; Rebel 15% credit; franchise derivative table.
